3. **Generate report** (format below)

## Cost Optimization Checklist

### Monthly Check

- [ ] Review free tier utilization (flag services above 80%)
- [ ] Check for unused services
- [ ] Check for abnormal increases in variable costs

### Quarterly Check

- [ ] Conduct full division cost audit
- [ ] Evaluate alternative services (cheaper options available?)
- [ ] Consider annual billing switches (when 20%+ discount vs. monthly)
- [ ] Recalculate break-even point (reflect cost changes)

### New Service Adoption Check

- [ ] Monthly cost estimate
- [ ] Free tier availability and limits
- [ ] Cost growth curve at scale
- [ ] Comparison with alternative services
- [ ] Ease of cancellation/migration
